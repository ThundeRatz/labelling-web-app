Image labelling app

Step 1

Open cone-labelling.herokuapp.com/label. You will see an image containing a cone, the image ID and a Submit button.

Step 2

Zoom in the page and carefully drag-and-drog a tight bounding box around the cone. Its base should be included if visible. You can remove previous boxes and redo if needed by clicking the X on the top right corner of the box.

Step 3

Click submit!
